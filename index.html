The opportunity

The window to lead is open. It will not stay open long.

Frontier AI is no longer a research curiosity. It is now the most leveraged tool a student entrepreneur can put their hands on — and the gap between students who use it well and those who don't is widening every semester.

Babson built its reputation on the belief that entrepreneurship is taught, practiced, and refined here better than anywhere else. That reputation is now being challenged by peers spending real money to put frontier models into their students' hands. Babson can move first, move cleanly, and stay distinctive — or watch peers define the next decade of entrepreneurship education while we read about it.

This site lays out the case in seven short pages: what the frontier looks like, who actually builds it, what a partnership costs, what other schools are doing, what Babson students are already building, and what it costs us if we wait.
