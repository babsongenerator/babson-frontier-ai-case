Student stories

Babson students are already building things they couldn't have built two years ago.

The Babson Build Fund backs roughly 500 student entrepreneurs each year. The G1000 initiative aims to grow that founder base by an order of magnitude. The students below are the leading edge of that population — what they built, how fast, and why the tools mattered.

A donor-facing note

The number of Babson grads starting companies today is small. The job is to grow it.

Frontier AI is the most direct lever Babson has to grow that number without growing headcount. Every vignette below is a student who, with the right tools in hand, shipped something a previous cohort would have spent a semester planning to start.
