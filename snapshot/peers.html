Different shapes, same direction

The shape varies. Wharton bought universal MBA seats. The California State University system signed the largest publicly-disclosed deal. Northeastern made a campus-wide commitment to Anthropic with at the center. Rice rolled out Gemini for Education across the whole campus. The shape is a choice; the direction is not. Babson has every advantage to make whatever choice fits — , the Weissman Foundry, the AI Innovators Bootcamp, and faculty already running the small-business AI pilot with Microsoft are all in place. What’s missing is the upstream relationship.
