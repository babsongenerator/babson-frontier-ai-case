Three companies. End of list.

The stack

What sits on top, and what sits underneath.

Two products often mentioned in the same breath as the frontier — Microsoft Copilot and Perplexity — are not foundation models. They are user-facing tools built on top of the frontier labs' models. That distinction matters when you're deciding where to invest.

Wrappers (downstream)
Microsoft Copilot Perplexity Cursor ChatGPT (consumer app) + thousands of others

These products add UX, retrieval, or workflow on top of base models. Useful — but their capabilities are bounded by the frontier labs they depend on.

Foundation labs (upstream — partner here)
Google · Gemini Anthropic · Claude OpenAI · GPT

These three labs train the models everything else depends on. Partner directly here and you get a seat at the table, not a seat in the audience.

DeepSeek is excluded from this analysis.

Capable models, important research — but a Chinese state-adjacent lab is not a candidate for an institutional partnership at Babson. We note them for completeness and move on.
